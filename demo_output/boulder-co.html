Do you treat trail running injuries common in Boulder?

Absolutely. Our therapists are trail running specialists who understand Boulder's unique terrain from Chautauqua to Green Mountain. We treat IT band syndrome, plantar fasciitis, and other overuse injuries common among local runners.

How quickly can I get an appointment at your Boulder location?

We offer same-day appointments for urgent needs and typically have availability within 24-48 hours for new patients. Call (303) 555-0198 or book online for fastest scheduling.

Do you accept my insurance?

Yes, we're in-network with all major insurance plans including Kaiser Permanente, Cigna, Anthem Blue Cross, United Healthcare, and Aetna. We'll verify your benefits before your first visit.

Is parking available near your Pearl Street clinic?

Yes, we have validated parking available in the Pearl Street Mall garage, plus street parking on Pearl Street and surrounding blocks. The clinic is also easily accessible by bike via Boulder's bike path system.

What should I bring to my first physical therapy appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, insurance card, and any recent imaging (X-rays, MRI) related to your condition. Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to the area being treated.
